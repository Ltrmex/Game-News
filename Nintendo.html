Target Says Wii U Was One Of The Top Sellers On Thanksgiving

US retailer Target has announced that it broke its online sales record on Thanksgiving. The company says this year’s sale has easily outpaced last year’s performance. The three top-selling products this year were the iPad, the Apple Watch and finally the Wii U. Let’s hope this trend continues for other retailers. The record-breaking sales online were driven by three items: the Apple iPad, the Apple Watch, and the Wii U gaming console, the company said.

The Frog Suit Costume Is Coming To Super Mario Maker
