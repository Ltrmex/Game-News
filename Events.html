Events

Gamescom 2016

Gamescom 2016 is a trade fair for video games at the Koelnmesse in Cologne, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. It is organized by the Bundesverband Interaktive Unterhaltungssoftware (Federal Association of Interactive Entertainment Software). It is used by many video game developers to show off their upcoming games and game-related hardware.


E3 2016

E3 2016 is the world’s premier trade show for computer and video games and related products. The most influential people leading the most innovative companies in the industry attend E3 to see groundbreaking new technologies and never-before-seen products for computers, video game consoles, handheld systems, mobile and social, and the Internet.


PAX East 2016

The Penny Arcade Expo (PAX East 2016) is an annual gamer festival held in Boston MA, USA. PAX was created by Jerry Holkins and Mike Krahulik, the authors of the Penny Arcade webcomic, because they wanted to attend a show that gave equal attention to console gamers, computer gamers, and tabletop gamers.


Comicpalooza 2016

Comicpalooza 2016 is the largest annual, multigenre, comic book, science fiction, anime, gaming and pop culture convention in Houston, Texas. The event is organized by Startling Events LLC. The event hosts the annual Comicpalooza Fandom Awards.
